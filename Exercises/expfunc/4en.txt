On May 1, a farm has about 200 chickens. The amount of chickens
increases by 20\% per week.

a) How many chickens are on the farm on June 1?

b) After how many days does the farm have more than 1000 chickens?

c) A competing farm increased its number of chickens from 200 to
over 1000 in only 43 days. By what percentage does the amount of
chickens increase per week there?
